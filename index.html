League of Legends

Difficulty:

If you are a League of Legends fan, you don't want to miss this theme. Take your time and see whether if you know everything about League of Legends lore. You will have a chance to be a part of the lore and become on of the character. Your job is to reach the end of the lore and survive by making the correct choice

List of puzzles type that might occured:

  • quizzes type
  • mutliple selection
  • pick the right order / combination
  • solve hidden messages
  • input the right answer in the box

Among Us Theme

Difficulty:

Fix the spaceship as a crewmember of the amongus spacecrew! There is an impostor among the crew, killing crewmembers and destroying the ship. Find the impostor and fix the ship before it's too late.

Your tasks are:

  • Find the impostor
  • Do your tasks
  • Vote the impostor off the ship
